About Me

I am a graduate student focusing on AI + Music. My research interests include Music Information Retreival Music Source Separation, Generative Music, and EEG. I aim to combine neural decoding with intelligent audio systems to explore brain-inspired human–computer interaction in music.

Keywords: EEG decoding, Attention, Source Separation, Audio DSP, Transformers, Diffusion, PyTorch, HPC

Projects

EGMP: EEG-Guided Music Perception

A two-stage system: Stage 1 predicts the attended instrument from EEG; Stage 2 selects precomputed masks to separate the corresponding source.

Tech: PyTorch · torchaudio · Conv/Attention · Sliding Window

BASEN Reproduction & Variants
